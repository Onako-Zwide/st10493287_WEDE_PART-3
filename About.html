About FreshMart ORGANICS

FreshMart ORGANICS is a retail organization founded in Grahamstown (now Makhanda) in 2017 by entrepreneurs, Onako Zwide & Alizwa Tshantshi. We aim to provide organic products that are sustainably grown, sold fresh, and affordable to promote healthy living among individuals.

We started as a small farmer’s market stall and have grown into a retail brand offering a variety of organic fruits, vegetables, dairy, and products. Our emphasis is “Organic” and we offer plant-based milk (oat/soy/almond), freshly squeezed juices, organic cheese and yoghurt, and organic skincare products (soaps, lotions, oils).

Our eco-friendly products include biodegradable cleaning products, reusable shopping bags, and kitchenware (spoons, plates, and straws).

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide organic products that are sustainably grown, sold fresh, and affordable to promote healthy living among individuals. We also utilize online shopping to simplify things for our customers to choose healthier lifestyles.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become South Africa’s favorite place to shop for healthy, organic, and eco-friendly products. We want to ensure that every individual has access to good quality food and sustainable items while helping protect the environment and supporting farmers.

Our Values

  • Quality: We are committed to providing the highest quality organic products that meet strict standards for health and sustainability.
  • Customer Focus: We prioritize our customers' needs and strive to offer exceptional service and a seamless shopping experience.
  • Sustainability: We believe in protecting the environment through eco-friendly practices and products that reduce waste and promote conservation.
  • Community: We support local farmers and artisans, fostering a sense of community and promoting economic growth in the areas we serve.
  • Integrity: We operate with honesty and transparency, building trust with our customers, suppliers, and partners.
